### Category 4: Content Accuracy (57% ❌) — 3 items to fix

**❌ FAIL: Taste bud misconception (page 11)**
"Your tongue has lots of tiny bumps. The bumps help you taste."
- The bumps are papillae; taste buds are inside them. Not all papillae contain taste buds (filiform papillae do not).
- **Recommended fix:** "Your tongue has tiny taste buds. They help you taste!"

**❌ FAIL: Taste bud count (page 11)**
"You have about eight thousand of them!"
- Children have approximately 10,000 taste buds. Decline doesn't begin until ~age 40.
- **Recommended fix:** "You have about ten thousand of them!"

**❌ FAIL: Core Knowledge scope alignment**
